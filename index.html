About

I am a technologist who loves to solve difficult problems and learn new things. Over the past 17 years I've built and managed a wide variety of software projects in multiple industries. I enjoy growing, mentoring, and leading teams as much as I like architecting nimble solutions to challenging projects.

Currently I am working on building the modern SaaS e-commerce platform Kibo Commerce which was formerly known as Mozu. Previously I've worked at Perficient technology consulting, at Dell creating a new search platform for Dell.com, and at Telligent building enterprise social collaboration software such as Community Server and Graffiti CMS.

I live in the beautiful and fun city of Austin, Texas. I'm a father; a geek; someone who loves to learn about everything and is inquisitive about the universe; a former Floridian; an aspiring runner; a space buff; a fan of pumpkins, tacos, Gators, and beer. I have a web site that I sometimes maintain at KevinHarder.com.
