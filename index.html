Howdy. I'm a technologist who loves to solve difficult problems and learn new things. Over the past 20 years I've built and managed a wide variety of software projects in multiple industries. I enjoy growing, mentoring, and leading teams as much as I like architecting nimble solutions to challenging projects. You can view my full professional experience on my LinkedIn profile.

I am an engineering manager in the Indeed Incubator, an internal new product accelerator at Indeed charged with pioneering new product ideas and assessing their viability through fast launches and empirical learning. I'm currently leading two Incubator teams. The first is Indeed Text2Apply, where we are building and scaling a new product that allows job seekers to easily apply via text message to job openings. The second is Indeed Virtual Evaluations - an experiment to help employers by doing screening and interviews on their behalf.

Previously, I spent five years building the modern SaaS e-commerce platform Kibo Commerce, which was formerly known as Mozu. Before that I worked at Dell where I built a new search platform for Dell.com, and at Telligent where I developed enterprise social collaboration software such as Community Server and Graffiti CMS.
