About

Professional

I am a technologist who loves to solve difficult problems and learn new things. Over the past 17 years I've built and managed a wide variety of software projects in multiple industries. I enjoy growing, mentoring, and leading teams as much as I like architecting nimble solutions to challenging projects. You can view my full professional experience on my LinkedIn profile.

Currently I am working on building the modern SaaS e-commerce platform Kibo Commerce, which was formerly known as Mozu. Previously I've worked at Dell where I built a new search platform for Dell.com, and at Telligent where I developed enterprise social collaboration software such as Community Server and Graffiti CMS.
